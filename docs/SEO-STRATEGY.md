# Praecora SEO strategy

Last updated 2026-05-18 — keyword research and internal linking strategy
for the Praecora field guide (the `/blog` content cluster).

This is the canonical reference for what each blog page is optimizing for,
which Tier 2 articles to write next, and how internal links should be
structured. Update it when the keyword data or article inventory changes
materially.

---

## Strategic frame (the why)

Praecora sells into a small, well-defined niche (independent music
catalog financing scouts). The direct keyword "music catalog scout" has
no measurable search volume — Google Keyword Planner doesn't even
expand it. **We cannot win organic from people who already know what
Praecora is.**

The blog strategy works because the *adjacent* keywords — operator pain
(IG bans, multiple accounts), tool comparisons (Pipedrive alternative,
ManyChat alternative), career/recruitment (music broker, how to become
A&R), and branded buyer research (beatBread, Chartmetric) — all have
meaningful search volume with low competition. The blog rank-bait
captures problem-aware and tool-shopping searchers and routes them to
Praecora once they discover the product fits their work.

**Goal:** demo bookings from problem-aware searchers within 90 days.
**Method:** topical authority via 25-article hub-and-spoke content
cluster across four pillars, each pillar hub-anchored to a high-volume
adjacent keyword.

## Internal linking — hub-and-spoke model

Every pillar has one **hub** article (the longest, highest-authority piece
targeting the pillar's primary keyword). All other articles in that
pillar are **spokes** that link back to the hub with anchor text matching
the hub's primary keyword.

### Pillar hubs

| Pillar | Hub article | Anchor text to use when linking |
|---|---|---|
| Broker | music-catalog-financing-broker-playbook | "music broker" |
| IG outreach | run-multiple-instagram-accounts-without-bans | "multiple instagram accounts" |
| Closing | cold-dm-indie-artists-instagram | "cold DM indie artists" / "cold DM Instagram" |
| Tools | best-crm-for-music-catalog-scouts | "music industry CRM" / "best CRM for music catalog scouts" |

### The five linking rules

1. **Every spoke links to its own hub** at least once, with anchor text
   matching the hub's primary keyword (exact match preferred).
2. **Every hub links to all its spokes** via a "Read next" section in
   prose, not just the auto-generated related-posts surface. Use
   spoke-primary-keyword anchor text.
3. **Cross-pillar links use exact-match secondary keywords** as anchor
   text. Never "click here," never "this article."
4. **The buyer directory is a cross-pillar hub** for brand-name authority.
   Every Broker-pillar article links to it; the catalog-financing-
   explained piece links to it twice (once mid-article, once in
   closing).
5. **Tier 2 articles link back to their hub AND to two adjacent
   spokes** at minimum. Three internal links per Tier 2 article is the
   floor.
